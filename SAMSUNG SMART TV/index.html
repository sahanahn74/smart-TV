About SAMSUNG SMART TV

A stunning TV with exceptional picture quality and smart features.

SAMSUNG Smart TV platform connects to your internet via either Wi-Fi or an Ethernet cable connection, a feature you enable during setup. This allows the Smart TV to run a wide variety of streaming apps, including popular options like Netflix or YouTube TV. But Samsung also has its own browser app, which functions like common browsers such as Firefox or Chrome, but it’s on your TV and controlled via your remote.
